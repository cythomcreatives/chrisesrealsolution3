Our Avocados

We offer premium organic avocados that are carefully handpicked, graded, and packed to meet international export standards.

Hass Avocados

Known for their rich, nutty flavor and creamy texture. Hass avocados have a long shelf life and are perfect for export.

  • Dark, pebbled skin when ripe
  • Creamy, pale green flesh
  • Rich, nutty flavor
  • Excellent shelf life

Fuerte Avocados

Fuerte avocados have a smooth, thin skin and a lighter flavor profile. They are known for their buttery texture.

  • Smooth, thin green skin
  • Buttery, creamy texture
  • Lighter, more delicate flavor
  • Medium to large size
