Green Coffee Bean Export

Premium Arabica and Robusta beans from Tanzania's finest regions

Service Overview

We partner with local farmers across Tanzania's prime coffee-growing regions to process and export high-quality Arabica and Robusta coffee beans to global markets. Our direct relationships with growers ensure traceability, fair pricing, and consistent quality that meets international standards.

With over 15 years of experience in the Tanzanian coffee industry, we've established a reputation for reliability and excellence. Our beans are carefully selected, processed, and packaged to preserve their unique flavor profiles that coffee connoisseurs worldwide have come to appreciate.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality control at every stage from farm to shipment. All beans are graded according to international standards.

Sustainable Sourcing

We work with farmers practicing sustainable agriculture, promoting environmental stewardship and community development.

Global Logistics

Efficient supply chain management ensures timely delivery to clients across Europe, North America, and Asia.

Our Export Process

1

Farmer Partnership & Selection

We establish partnerships with certified growers in Tanzania's prime coffee regions, including Kilimanjaro, Arusha, and Mbeya. Each farmer undergoes a rigorous selection process to ensure they meet our quality and sustainability standards.

2

Harvesting & Initial Processing

Our agronomists work closely with farmers during harvesting to ensure optimal picking times. Beans are then processed using either the wet or dry method, depending on the desired flavor profile and customer requirements.

3

Quality Control & Grading

At our processing facilities, beans undergo meticulous sorting, grading, and cupping tests conducted by certified Q-graders. We classify beans according to size, density, and defect count to meet specific market requirements.

4

Packaging & Documentation

Green coffee beans are packaged in grain-pro bags or vacuum-sealed containers to preserve freshness. We handle all export documentation, including certificates of origin, phytosanitary certificates, and customs clearance.

5

Shipping & Delivery

We coordinate logistics with trusted shipping partners to ensure timely delivery. Clients receive tracking information and regular updates throughout the shipping process until the beans reach their destination.
