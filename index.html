About Us

Learn about our history, values, and commitment to excellence

Chrises Real Solutions Company Limited is a diversified agribusiness and investment solutions company based in Tanzania. We specialize in high-quality agricultural production and exports, with a strong focus on sustainable farming practices and international trade.

Our mission

To connect Tanzania's rich agricultural resources and investment opportunities with international markets, particularly China, while ensuring quality, transparency, and sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading and trusted provider of agricultural exports and investment solutions from Tanzania to the global market.

Our Stratergies

  • To deliver premium-quality agricultural products to international clients.
  • To promote Tanzania's agricultural potential on the global stage.
  • To provide investors with secure and profitable land opportunities all over Tanzania.
  • To build lasting business relationships through transparency and efficiency.
